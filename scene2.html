Engine Cylinders in Gasoline Cars - 2017

  • The majority of gasoline cars have 4 cylinder engines, providing the best balance between MPG and horsepower
  • As manufacturers increase the number of cylinders the Overall MPG declines
  • Fewer cars are made with a large amount of cylinders (10-12), a trend that has continued over time
